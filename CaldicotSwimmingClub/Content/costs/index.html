When you join the club you will be given a free trial period of two weeks, before the following payments are due. These payments allow you to come to any of the applicable sessions.

The annual fee is made up of Swim Wales registration and club fees, payable in the following manner:
  • The club charges a flat rate of £20 pounds per month working out at less than 2 pound per session.
  • There is also an annual payment that needs to be paid by the 3rd Tuesday in march which covers insurance and Swim Wales registration. These payments vary and are shown bellow.
    • For those born before 1st April 2002
      • 1st member - £42
      • 2nd member - £35
      • 3rd member - £31
    • For those born on or after 1st April 2002
      • 1st member - £40
      • 2nd member - £33
      • 3rd member - £29

The monthly fees are payable by standing order on the first of the month or can be paid at the desk(Tuesday evenings).

Masters
The same fees apply for the masters.

Improvers
For Improvers there is a reduced monthly fee of ten pound as they only meet once a week at the shallow end of the pool.
